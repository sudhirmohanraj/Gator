Gator-Translator

App to translate from Malayalam to English and vice versa.

Services Supported
	1. Malayalam<->English a single phrase or word translation.
	2. If a word does not exist in the internal word look up;then return a message to the user Stating the given word could not be translated.

Limitations
	Only can translate one phrase or a word at a time.
